Key Success Stories

As part of my work with one of the government departments of Zimbabwe, I had the opportunity to design and implement complex ETL processes from 20+ Data Sources, build robust database systems, and develop cutting-edge tools for real-time and offline analytics using PowerBI and Microsoft SSRS. I also took ownership of troubleshooting software and processes to ensure data consistency and integrity using constraints and SQL quiries that constantly tested data regularly for incomplete or redundant entries. One of my key contributions was leading the design and integration of vast, complex data sets from multiple sources, which enabled our business partners to gain valuable insights and make informed decisions.

I was tasked with developing a predictive analytics model for a health Insurance. The goal was to help the company manage its utilization and costs more effectively. After analyzing the data, I was able to provide the insurer with valuable insights into the top and bottom 5 sectors that had made claims using SQL and SSRS Reports.Based on this information, the company was able to refocus its business strategy and make more informed decisions about where to allocate resources. As a result, the insurer was able to reduce claims by 12%, which had a significant impact on its bottom line.

I worked on a project for a supermarket where I created a data warehouse and implemented an analytics solution. The goal was to identify customer spending patterns, using SQL and crystal reports and help the supermarket make data-driven decisions. The project was a success and resulted in a 13% increase in sales revenue. The data warehouse allowed the supermarket to collect and store large amounts of data from various sources. The analytics solution then analyzed this data to provide insights into customer behavior, preferences, and trends. With this information, the supermarket was able to optimize its product offerings, pricing strategies, and marketing campaigns. Overall, the project demonstrated the power of data analytics and how it can drive business growth and success.
